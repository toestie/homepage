Food Delivery Analysis

Earthquake Visualization

"Travel Index"

We scraped data from MasterCard's Index of Global Destination Cities and the World Population review to analyze correlations between popular tourist destinations and other metrics. We found interesting trends such as "freedom of choice" not correlating with population happiness as well as Asian cities being the most visited as well as least expensive (generally) vacation spots.

Automated Excel Gradebook

An Excel notebook designed to automatically grade exams and manage a gradebook. Also provides per question and per exam statistics as well as grade overviews for students to track their own progress. Macros intended to integrate with imported scans from Zipgrade.
